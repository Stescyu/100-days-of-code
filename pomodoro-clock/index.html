Pomodoro Clock

What is a Pomodoro Clock?

The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

Instructions:

Insert your desired work time (default is 25 minutes), and it will start counting down on start, after the timer reaches 0, there will be an indication for a 5 minute break. Every 4 cycles, you will receive a 15 minute break.
